ESRC(Robotics Club)

It is one of the finest clubs of the college and is under the leadership of Dr. Alok Singh Gahlot (M.S.;B.tech) . Under his guidance, the club has organised various workshops for the students. It is the interest and passion of Dr. Alok singh Gahlot that the club is able to teach and help the students in the on growing field of ROBOTICS. This Club was founded by some of the ex-students with Dr. Alok taking under the consideration, the vast field of ROBOTICS. The robotics club plans to host events in which students can learn about the fundamentals of robotics and work in a friendly environment among helpful peers. Our members will work in teams to design, build, and program robots. These teams will then pit their robots against one another in competitions that will take place on campus. These Events will be open to all students and staff to enjoy.


IPC(Innovation and Programming Club)

Programming Club aims to provide a nurturing environment for students from all streams and courses to develop their interest and skills in computer programming. The Club focuses equally on competitive programming as well as extra-curricular software projects. Club welcomes ideas, suggestions and sponsorships from faculty members, departments and industries to encourage its members. The Club is open to all segments of programming including desktop, UI/UX, Web, Mobile, Open Source, Gaming, AI, HCI etc. Target of this club is to build best personal skills of our students on computer programming for national, regional & international programming contest with a strong focus on learning and collaboration. Apart from studies this club gives students the opportunity to show their talent.


Vision:


To create a continuously growing community of individuals (students/scholars/faculties) who are capable of (or willing to) dive into the art of computer programming.

Mission:


1. To motivate members towards computer programming via multiple platforms/channels.
2. To encourage students for participation in competitive programming.
3. To provide support and expertise for representing the college at national/international programming contests.
4. To create a programming and problem-solving culture among students right from the start.
5. To provide problems, approaches and support for developing software projects.
6. To provide exposure to latest technologies and platforms used by the industry.
7. To create a channel for frequent communication between current students and alumni currently working in IT organizations.
